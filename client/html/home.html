Pooxle

Bored? Draw some pixels.

Instructions

  1. Hover your mouse cursor over the gridbox you want to place a pixel in
  2. Click the left mouse button to place a pixel in that gridbox
  3. To continuously place pixels, hold down the left mouse button
  4. Add a caption describing your pooxle
  5. Add a name so everyone knows who made this pooxle
  6. Click the share button to post your pooxle for everyone to see
  7. If the canvas does not appear, refresh the page

Find a person's pooxle by searching their name here

  1. Enter the name of the pooxler you wish to look for in the search bar
  2. Click the search button to find their pooxle
  3. To remove the search filter, refresh the page
  4. By default, all pooxles are shown whenever the page is loaded
