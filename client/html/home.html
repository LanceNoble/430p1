Pooxle

Bored? Draw some pixels.

Instructions

  1. To place a pixel, left click on the desired gridbox you wish to place a pixel in
  2. To continuously place pixels, hold down the left mouse button
  3. Add a caption describing your pooxle and a name
  4. Click the share button to post your pooxle for everyone to see
  5. To update an existing pooxle, enter an existing name
  6. If the canvas does not appear, refresh the page

Find a person's pooxle by searching their name here

  1. Enter the name of the pooxler you wish to look for in the search bar
  2. Click the search button to find their pooxle
  3. To remove the search filter, refresh the page
  4. By default, all pooxles are shown whenever the page is loaded
